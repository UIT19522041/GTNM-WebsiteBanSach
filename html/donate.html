About Booksafe Donation Program

Our program is for anyone who would love to repurpose books they no longer want or need on their shelves. You will send us books, and in turn we donate them to charities to help children in need of school supplies and textbooks. BookSafe receives good quality, used books, sorts them, and finds new readers worldwide, extending the life of books. We put quality, affordable books into the hands of those who love to read.

FAQ

If you're having any problems or questions about the program, consult our FAQ.

  • Does this program cost any money?

    Absolutely nothing! We don't charge money when you're giving from the heart.

  • How do we get the books to you?

    We offer several shipping options depending on your volume and facility. We use several delivery methods including LTL freight services.

  • What types of books do you take?

    We sell on all the major marketplaces such as Amazon.com, Abe.com, Alibris.com, eBay.com, bn.com (Barnes & Noble), BookSafe.com, and many others.

  • Can we bring the books to you?

    Absolutely! Our large network of processing centers makes it easy and convenient for libraries to drop off their books.

  • Do you take books without an ISBN?

    Yes. We offer a special service where we evaluate and list non-ISBN, collectable, rare and antiquarian books.
